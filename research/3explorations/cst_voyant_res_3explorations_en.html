CLARIN-DK offers a data visualization tool, VoyantTools, an online environment that performs automatic text analysis with functionalities such as word frequency lists, frequency distribution plots, and KWIC(KeyWordInContext) displays. It is an easy entry point to the use of digital methodologies for non-computational researchers and teachers.

The Brandes corpus is loaded into VoyantTools when accessing this link.

VoyantTools are developed by Stefan Sinclair & Geoffrey Rockwell (https://voyant-tools.org/docs).
