Georg Brandes Timeline 1842-1927 (1998)

by Per Dahl

This work was originally published as no 48 in the Working Papers series from the Department of Literary History, University of Aarhus. It has been the foundation for the digital timeline (see 'Tools'), which, in dialogue with the author, has been translated into English, updated and focused on Georg Brandes' international activities by Jens Bjerring-Hansen and Ida Marie Klahn.
